At Cboe Global Markets, I conduct end-to-end cross-market surveillance across options and futures, using Nasdaq SMARTS alongside Python, SQL, and TB-scale Snowflake pipelines. That dual background in investigation and institutional compliance is what I bring to every engagement.

I believe rigorous surveillance isn't just about catching bad actors. It's about building systems precise enough to distinguish intent from noise, at scale, under scrutiny.
