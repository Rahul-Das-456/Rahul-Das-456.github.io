Search 1 · 2017 – 2019

The Volume Play — 4,000 Applications

Fresh out of undergrad, no compliance-specific brand, no network. Solved it the only way that felt controllable: apply everywhere, every day. The result was exhausting, humbling, and ultimately worked — but the math was brutal.

Search 2 · 2023 – 2024

The Targeted Play — 300 Applications

This time: SIE certified, CAPM in hand, 4+ years of compliance experience, and a deliberate list of target firms. Applied only to roles with a referral, a direct recruiter connection, or a strong profile match. 93% fewer applications. Same number of acceptances.

Volume vs Targeting

Ghosted rate dropped from 90% to 50% simply by narrowing scope. A referral or recruiter relationship cut the chance of disappearing into an ATS to near zero.

Funnel Shape

Search 1's funnel was extremely narrow at the top and nearly invisible by final rounds. Search 2 maintained consistent conversion at every stage — a signal of better fit, not better luck.

Same Output, Fraction of the Cost

Both searches ended with 3 accepted roles. Search 2 took 13× fewer applications to get there — meaning 3,700 fewer rejections, fewer months, and far less emotional overhead.

Compliance Market Lesson

Compliance hiring is heavily relationship-driven. The credential gap (no SIE, no certs) in Search 1 couldn't be overcome with volume — but could be overcome with credentials and targeted positioning in Search 2.
